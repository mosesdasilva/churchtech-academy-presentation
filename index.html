Technology training for churches, built with spiritual clarity and practical excellence.

ChurchTech Academy exists to give churches accessible, free, and spiritually aligned production training for livestreams, audio, media, and service support teams.

External Environmental Scan

Different church sizes share the same problem: production training gaps

Small Churches

Volunteers often lead with little training, limited time, and minimal budget.

  • Step-by-step onboarding is critical
  • Repeatable systems matter
  • Paid training is often out of reach

Medium Churches

Infrastructure exists, but volunteer consistency and technical depth are uneven.

  • Teams need scalable training
  • Livestream quality matters
  • Audio clarity and reliability are essential

Large Churches

Staff may exist, but volunteer onboarding and standardization are ongoing needs.

  • Role-based pathways help
  • Libraries and benchmarks improve quality
  • Strong churches can influence others

Materials And Facilities

A lightweight but real ministry production setup supports the training model.

  • Production equipment: cameras, microphones, switcher or console access, lighting examples, and related church tech gear
  • Teaching equipment: laptop, presentation display, internet access, microphones, and slide support
  • Course materials: lesson outlines, handouts, note sheets, checklists, pathways, and completion certificates
  • Facilities: recording space, editing workspace, and access to real church environments for demonstrations
  • Platform materials: hosting, course pages, logins, multilingual assets, and communication systems

Evaluation

Evaluation that keeps the platform aligned, effective, and healthy.

Review Cadence

Evaluation begins after the pilot phase and first course releases generate usable feedback.

Weekly

Informal internal review of production quality, content progress, and workflow bottlenecks.

Monthly

Review participant engagement, lesson completion, church feedback, and platform performance.

Quarterly

Formal review of course effectiveness, strategic goals, and ministry impact.

Annually

High-level review of mission alignment, funding sustainability, curriculum growth, and expansion priorities.
